Eazy Rent

EazyRent is a university project i contributed in along side two of my classmates
for a vehicule renting website. It is a website that makes finding a rental vehicule a lot easier. With offers
from partnered and verified agencies to make renting a vehicule safe and secure implemented Face
recognition and other AI features.

Client

A Client is presented with offers from nearest Agencies. Offers can be filtered to the desired
vehicules and features and a pick-up and drop-off location so the client receives the vehicule from anywhere.

Agency

Each agency has an Owner, Secretaries and Garage managers. Each one has their own interface
with multiple functionalities and roles.

An aganecy can manage their reservation requests and check the details of each one
as well as client information.
